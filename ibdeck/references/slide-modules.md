# IBDeck Slide Modules

Use these modules as reusable building blocks.

## Executive Summary

Purpose: answer the core question immediately.

Structure: title, 3-5 message boxes, optional implications row, source footer.

Use for: first substantive slide, board deck, investor presentation.

## Situation-Complication-Question

Purpose: frame why the topic matters now.

Structure: three columns or stacked blocks; each block has evidence and implication.

Use for: strategy, market entry, transaction rationale.

## Market Sizing

Purpose: quantify opportunity.

Structure: TAM/SAM/SOM, bottom-up drivers, CAGR bridge, source notes.

Use for: industry research, investor decks, market entry.

## Growth Driver Matrix

Purpose: compare drivers by magnitude and certainty.

Structure: rows for drivers; columns for evidence, impact, timing, confidence.

Use for: industry analysis and investment theses.

## Competitive Landscape

Purpose: show positioning.

Structure: 2x2, bubble map, share table, or capability matrix.

Use for: company profiles, market maps, acquisition screening.

## Company Snapshot

Purpose: summarize a company quickly.

Structure: business overview, key metrics, products, customers, ownership, recent developments.

Use for: target profile, buyer/seller pitch, appendix.

## Financial Overview

Purpose: present operating and financial trajectory.

Structure: revenue/EBITDA chart, margin bridge, KPI table, commentary.

Use for: investor presentation, valuation, transaction overview.

## Valuation Football Field

Purpose: show valuation range from multiple methods.

Structure: method rows with low/high bars, implied midpoint, key assumptions.

Use for: M&A, fairness, capital markets.

## Transaction Comparables

Purpose: benchmark multiples and deal precedent.

Structure: table with target, acquirer, date, EV, revenue/EBITDA multiples, strategic notes.

Use for: valuation and market appetite.

## Public Comparables

Purpose: benchmark listed peers.

Structure: table with market cap, EV, growth, margins, multiples, notes.

Use for: valuation, investor positioning.

## Timeline / Roadmap

Purpose: sequence milestones.

Structure: horizontal phases, milestones, owners, dependencies.

Use for: implementation plans, transaction process, capital raise timeline.

## Risk Register

Purpose: make risks explicit and mitigable.

Structure: risk, evidence, impact, likelihood, mitigation.

Use for: investment committee, board, diligence.

## Recommendation / Next Steps

Purpose: close with action.

Structure: recommendation box, 3-5 next steps, decision asks, timeline.

Use for: final slide before appendix.
