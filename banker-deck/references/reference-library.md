# BankerDeck Reference Library

Use this as a source planning guide. Browse or fetch current data when facts may have changed.

## Company and Financial Data

- Public filings: SEC EDGAR, HKEX, SSE/SZSE, company annual reports, investor relations decks.
- Market data: exchange websites, Yahoo Finance/Google Finance for quick checks, paid terminals if user has access.
- Company facts: official websites, press releases, investor presentations, audited reports.

## Industry and Market Research

- Government/statistical sources: World Bank, IMF, OECD, national statistics bureaus, customs/trade databases.
- Sector regulators: energy, telecom, healthcare, transport, finance regulators.
- Industry associations: market size, shipment, capacity, standards, membership data.
- Reputable consulting/research publications: use selectively and cite clearly.

## Transactions and Valuation

- Press releases and deal announcements.
- Company filings around acquisitions, disposals, financings.
- Public comparable filings and market data.
- Transaction databases if available; otherwise label manual public-source screen.

## Source Notes

Short source footer format:

`Source: Company filings; industry association; BankerDeck analysis.`

For charts:

`Note: Market size in nominal USD; 2026E-2030E based on stated assumptions. Source: [sources]; BankerDeck analysis.`

For placeholders:

`Source: Placeholder pending verified market data.`

## Data Hygiene

- Show units and period on every quantitative exhibit.
- Distinguish reported data, estimated data, and BankerDeck analysis.
- Avoid implying proprietary data access unless the user provided it.
- For recent prices, leaders, laws, forecasts, and market statistics, verify with current sources.
