# BankerDeck Cases

## Case 1: Market Entry Deck

User input:

`Use BankerDeck to create a 12-page deck on whether a US renewable developer should enter the Vietnam battery storage market.`

Interpretation:

- Reader: management/investment committee.
- Decision: market entry and prioritization.
- Story: demand growth is attractive, but grid interconnection, offtake structure, and policy timing define entry route.
- Theme: `ib-classic` or `mckinsey-inspired`.

Suggested outline:

1. Executive summary with recommendation.
2. Why Vietnam battery storage matters now.
3. Power demand and renewable penetration outlook.
4. Storage market sizing and scenarios.
5. Policy and tariff framework.
6. Competitive and partnership landscape.
7. Project economics and sensitivity drivers.
8. Entry options: acquire, partner, greenfield.
9. Risk register and mitigants.
10. Recommended entry roadmap.
11. Data needs and diligence plan.
12. Appendix: assumptions and sources.

Generation approach:

- Use executive summary, market sizing, competitive landscape, financial overview, risk register, roadmap, and appendix modules.
- Add placeholders for market size and tariff values if not verified.

## Case 2: M&A Target Profile

User input:

`Generate a Goldman-style editable PPT for a buyer considering acquisition of a vertical SaaS company in healthcare.`

Interpretation:

- Reader: buyer corporate development team.
- Decision: whether to prioritize the target.
- Theme: `goldman-inspired`.

Suggested outline:

1. Target attractiveness summary.
2. Company snapshot.
3. Product and customer overview.
4. Revenue quality and KPI profile.
5. Market backdrop and growth drivers.
6. Competitive positioning.
7. Strategic fit with buyer.
8. Valuation football field.
9. Comparable companies.
10. Key diligence questions.

Generation approach:

- Use company snapshot, financial overview, growth driver matrix, valuation football field, public comps, and risk register modules.

## Case 3: Chinese Industry Research

User input:

`帮我用 BankerDeck 做一个中金风格的人形机器人产业链研究 PPT 大纲。`

Interpretation:

- Reader: investor or corporate strategy team in China.
- Output: research outline first, not necessarily full PPTX.
- Theme: `cicc-inspired`.
- Language: Chinese.

Suggested outline:

1. 核心结论：产业链从硬件降本转向整机能力验证。
2. 行业催化：政策、AI 模型、零部件成熟度。
3. 市场空间测算：出货量、ASP、场景渗透。
4. 产业链拆解：上游零部件、中游整机、下游应用。
5. 关键零部件格局：减速器、丝杠、电机、传感器。
6. 竞争格局：国内外公司定位矩阵。
7. 商业化节奏：工业、服务、家庭场景。
8. 投资主线与风险提示。

Generation approach:

- Use market sizing, growth driver matrix, competitive landscape, timeline, and risk register modules.
